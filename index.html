Somerville Fab Lab Programming

Complementing what exists

Somerville Fab will be part of a rich, maker community that already exists in and around Somerville.

This means that, in our programming, we should focus on:

  1. providing many points of entry for different people more than supporting specific communities.
  2. design, education, and entrepreneurship more than directly supporting artists or small business owners.
  3. (the design) process and projects more than the technical tools in the space.
  4. divergent output more than engraved nametags.
